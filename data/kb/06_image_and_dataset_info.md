## About HAM10000 Dataset

- HAM10000 (Human Against Machine with 10000 training images) is a large collection of dermatoscopic images.
- Images were collected from different populations (Australia, Austria) over 20 years.
- Contains 7 diagnostic categories: melanoma, nevus, BCC, actinic keratosis, benign keratosis, dermatofibroma, vascular lesions.
- Gold standard: histopathology (biopsy), expert consensus, or follow-up confirmation.
- Used to train and validate AI-based dermoscopy classification models.
