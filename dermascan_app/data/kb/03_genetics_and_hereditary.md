## Mutation-Specific Information

- **CDKN2A**: Most common hereditary melanoma gene. Carriers have up to 70% lifetime melanoma risk.
- **BAP1**: Associated with uveal melanoma, mesothelioma, and cutaneous BAP1-inactivated melanocytic tumors.
- **MC1R**: Red hair/fair skin gene. Doubles melanoma risk even without sun exposure.
- **BRCA2**: Primarily breast/ovarian cancer gene but also linked to increased melanoma risk.
- **TP53**: Li-Fraumeni syndrome gene. Broad cancer predisposition including skin cancers.

## Hereditary Risk Score Interpretation

- Score 0.0–0.3: LOW hereditary risk
- Score 0.3–0.6: MODERATE hereditary risk — annual screening recommended
- Score 0.6–0.8: HIGH hereditary risk — biannual dermatology visits
- Score 0.8–1.0: VERY HIGH — consider genetic counseling and prophylactic measures
