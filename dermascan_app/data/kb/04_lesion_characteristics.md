## ABCDE Rule for Melanoma Detection

- **A – Asymmetry**: One half doesn't match the other. Score >0.5 warrants concern.
- **B – Border**: Irregular, ragged, or blurred edges. Border irregularity >0.6 is suspicious.
- **C – Color**: Multiple shades of brown, black, red, pink, white, or blue.
- **D – Diameter**: Larger than 6mm (size of a pencil eraser). Lesions >10mm need evaluation.
- **E – Evolution**: Any change in size, shape, color, or new symptom like bleeding.
